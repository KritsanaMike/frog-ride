Help & FAQ

How do I request a ride?

To request a ride, go to the Home screen and tap the "Request Ride" button. Fill in your start and end locations, select the trip type, vehicle, and other details, then submit your request.

How is the fare calculated?

Fares are calculated based on the distance of the trip, the type of vehicle selected, and the duration for certain trip types. Inter-state trips may have different rate structures. You will see an estimated fare before confirming your request.

Can I cancel a ride?

Yes, you can cancel a ride. Cancellation policies and potential fees may apply depending on how close to the pickup time you cancel. Check the "Upcoming Trips" section in your history for cancellation options.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile by navigating to the "Profile" tab from the bottom navigation, then tapping the "Edit Profile" button.

What types of vehicles are available?

We offer a range of vehicles including Motorbikes, Sedans, Buses (16 seaters), and 4x4 Runners (Hilux, Jeep, Pick-up, Truck). Availability may vary by zone.
