Flog Ride is dedicated to providing safe, fast, and reliable transportation and accommodation solutions specifically tailored for NGO field work in Nigeria. Our platform connects NGO staff with vetted drivers and reputable hotels to ensure seamless and secure travel for important field operations.

Our mission is to support the vital work of NGOs by removing logistical hurdles, allowing them to focus on their core objectives of making a positive impact in communities across Nigeria.
