Privacy Policy

Welcome to Flog Ride's Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

This includes: Name, Organizational Email, Organization Name, Location, Department, Contact Number, Office Address, etc.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information collected via our app for a variety of business purposes described below. We process your personal information for these purposes in reliance on our legitimate business interests, in order to enter into or perform a contract with you, with your consent, and/or for compliance with our legal obligations. These include: to facilitate account creation, to send administrative information, to manage user accounts, to deliver services to the user, etc.

4. Will Your Information Be Shared?

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. For example, sharing driver details with passengers for a booked ride.

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.
