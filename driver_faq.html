Driver Help & FAQ

How do I go online/offline?

On the main Dashboard screen, use the toggle switch at the top to set your status to "Online" (to receive ride requests) or "Offline" (to stop receiving requests).

How do I accept a ride request?

When a new ride request appears on your Dashboard, tap "View Details". Review the information and then tap "Accept Ride".

When and how do I get paid?

Your earnings are calculated per trip, minus any applicable service fees. Payouts are typically processed on a weekly basis to your registered bank account. You can view your earnings summary and transaction history in the "Earnings" tab.

What if I have an issue during a trip?

For emergencies, use the "Emergency" button on the navigation screen. For other issues, you can contact support through the "Submit Feedback / Report Issue" option in Settings, or call the support line if available.

How can I update my vehicle or document information?

Go to your "Profile" tab, then tap "Edit Profile". You can update your vehicle details and upload new documents there. Some changes may require admin approval.
